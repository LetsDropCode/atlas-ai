Evidence over opinion – recommendations should be backed by data whenever possible.
Value over price – optimise for overall value, not simply the lowest cost.
Calm over complexity – reduce cognitive load; don't overwhelm users.
Explain before recommending – every recommendation should be understandable.
Privacy by default – collect the minimum data needed and be transparent about its use.
Composable architecture – every module should be replaceable without rewriting the platform.
Human-in-the-loop – Atlas advises; users decide.
Continuous improvement – every trip should make the platform smarter.
Global by design – never assume one country, currency, language or travel style.
Delight through details – the small touches (like sunrise timing, hidden cafés or avoiding crowds) are what make Atlas memorable.
