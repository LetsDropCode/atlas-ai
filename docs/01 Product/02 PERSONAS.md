Primary Personas
Atlas AI is designed around four core traveller personas.
Persona 1 — Luxury Value Traveller
Example:
Lindsay & Kendall
Goals:
Luxury experiences
Boutique accommodation
Excellent food
Photography
Good walking locations
Strong value for money
Pain Points:
Too many booking websites
Hard to know if something is genuinely a good deal
Fear of booking too early or too late
Atlas Helps By:
Experience Value Engine
Regret Engine
Booking Confidence
Itinerary Intelligence
Persona 2 — Adventure Explorer
Goals:
Nature
Hiking
Local experiences
Flexible itineraries
Budget optimisation
Atlas Helps By:
Alternative destinations

Activity recommendations
Safety guidance
Persona 3 — Family Planner
Goals:
Convenience
